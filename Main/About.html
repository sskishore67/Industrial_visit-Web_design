Industrial Visit

About us

Industrial visit has its own place of importance in a career of a student who is pursuing a professional degree course like BBA. In fact, it constitutes a part of core course curriculum taught at the college. The objective of an industrial visit is to provide the students an insight regarding the internal working of companies. Today, we all perfectly understand that theoretical knowledge is just not enough for a successful professional career. It is here that by going beyond academics, industrial visit provides students a much relevant practical perspective of the actual work place and the larger world of business. All this makes the importance of Industrial visits all too evident, especially for students pursuing a professional management course like BBA.
