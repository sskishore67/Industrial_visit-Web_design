Tour packages can be booked after registering a user id and password or can also be booked using the guest login.

The contact details are available while booking the respective packages in the “View Details” option.

Apart from the contact numbers displayed in the respective packages, the toll-free number 000000000 can also be used.

All queries regarding booking/cancellation of tour packages or services can be mailed to Industrailvisit@gmail.com.

After login in the portal, click on the required icon in the home page which will take you to the respective package. Then click the “Details” tab, where you will get the Overview,Terms and Conditions and Contact us. You will get the day-wise itinerary when you click the Book Button.

All Tour packages including services like Retiring room, Hotels, Lounge, etc can be booked on the portal.

The packages can be booked using the option of “International Cards”

  • NEFT/RTGS
  • Debit/Credit Card
  • Wallets (PayTm, Razorpay)
  • Cash
  • Cheque (subject to realization)
  • DD
  • BHIM/UPI
